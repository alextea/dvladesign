Other ways to apply

By phone

  • Telephone: 0300 123 4321
  • Textphone: 0300 790 6201

Calls to 03 numbers cost approximately:

  • 2p to 10p from landlines
  • 10p to 40p from mobile phones

Find out more about call charges

At the Post Office

Go to your local Post Office that deals with vehicle tax. You'll need to take:

  • completed V11 reminder (or your V5C)
  • MOT test certificate if required (must be valid when the tax starts)
  • the payment shown on the reminder

In Northern Ireland you'll also need an insurance certificate or cover note.

Get a V5C vehicle registration certificate (log book)
If you have lost the V5C with your name on, you can get a replacement by filling in a V62 form.
